# Support

Use GitHub issues for project questions, bug reports, accessibility reports,
source corrections, and feature requests.

Maintainers and contributors do not provide legal advice, representation,
regulated advice, crisis support, or casework. The tools provide general
information and drafting support only.

For urgent legal, housing, health, safeguarding, immigration, benefits, debt, or
similar issues, contact an appropriate qualified adviser, emergency service,
specialist charity, ombudsman, regulator, or public body.

For security or privacy concerns, follow `SECURITY.md`.
