FOI basics

20 working days

Public authorities normally have 20 working days to respond. Working days exclude weekends and bank holidays.

Request in writing

Send by email or letter. Include your name, an address for the reply, and a clear description of the information you want.

Internal review first

If the deadline passes or you are unhappy with the response, ask the authority for an internal review before complaining to the ICO.

Plain-English safety note

This is a tracking aid, not legal advice. Verify deadlines, exemptions, and routes against the current GOV.UK and ICO guidance.

Contribute

Useful improvements include more authority-type guidance, tested escalation letters, calendar reminder formats, and accessibility fixes. Keep the project static and avoid collecting personal data.
