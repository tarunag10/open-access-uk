# Maintainers

Current maintainer:

- `@tarunag10`

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing code, accessibility, privacy, and source-risk changes.
- Keeping the static/browser-only architecture clear.
- Triaging security, privacy, and misleading-guidance reports.
- Maintaining root governance, issue templates, and release notes.
- Ensuring high-risk legal and public-service content has source provenance.

## Review Expectations

At least one maintainer should review changes that affect:

- Legal or public-service guidance.
- Complaint routes, ombudsman routing, deadlines, or formal letter wording.
- Privacy or data-handling behaviour.
- Security headers, dependency policy, or deployment configuration.
- Accessibility patterns, forms, or keyboard flows.
