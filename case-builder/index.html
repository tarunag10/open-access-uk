How a case file helps

Portable

Export your case as a JSON file. Import it back later, or share it with an adviser for handoff.

Connected

Bring together letters, evidence, deadlines, and journey steps in one place, even if they came from different tools.

Private

Your case data stays in your browser unless you choose to export it. Nothing is sent to a server.

Plain-English safety note

This is a case management aid, not legal advice. Verify deadlines, routes, and exemptions against current GOV.UK and specialist guidance.

Contribute

Useful improvements include more issue-category templates, more export formats, and accessibility fixes. Keep the project static and avoid collecting personal data.
