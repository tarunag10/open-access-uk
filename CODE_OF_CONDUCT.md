# Code of Conduct

Open Access UK is for people improving civic access, public-service usability,
and open-source public-interest tooling.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and patient.
- Use plain language where possible.
- Assume people have different technical, legal, accessibility, and lived
  experience.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Protect privacy and do not ask contributors to disclose unnecessary personal
  or sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, threats, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing another person's private information without consent.
- Dismissing accessibility, safety, or privacy concerns without review.
- Presenting legal or public-service information as professional advice.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that breach this code. Serious or repeated breaches may lead to
temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report concerns using the support and security routes in `SUPPORT.md` and
`SECURITY.md`.
